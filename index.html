Analyze your LinkedIn connections to gain insights into your professional network.

How to Get Started

  1. Export Your LinkedIn Connections:
    • Go to LinkedIn and click on "Settings & Privacy"
    • Navigate to "Data privacy" section
    • Click "Get a copy of your data"
    • Select "Connections" and request your archive
    • LinkedIn will email you a download link (usually within 10 minutes)
  2. Download the CSV File: Once you receive the email, download the Connections.csv file to your computer.
  3. Upload to Analyzer: Click on "Upload CSV" in the menu and upload your Connections.csv file.
  4. Analyze Your Data: Once uploaded, explore the Analysis and Summary pages to gain insights into your network.

What You'll Discover

This tool helps you understand:

  • Your most common connections by first name
  • Top companies in your network
  • Most common job positions
  • Connection growth over time
  • Recent networking activity
  • Searchable database of all your connections

Privacy Note: All data processing happens locally in your browser. Your connection data is never uploaded to any server.
